em vs rem (compounding)

1.2em here (19.2px, relative to body's 1rem)
1.2em here too (23.04px — relative to the PARENT's 19.2px, not the root)
1.2em again (27.65px — compounds further)
1.2rem here (always 19.2px — rem ignores nesting, relative to root only)
